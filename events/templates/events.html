Featured Events

Engineer's Day

An event to facilitate and encourage the top performers of each department and year.

September 17, 2018 Main Seminar Hall, NITK

RTFM (Read the F#@cking Manual)

This is an event conducted by IE Code under the flagship event Enigma. Here, first years gather to test their wits in a series of technical Computer Science events. Participants are given a manual with the help of which they have to solve a series of tasks.

Sync

Event in which students become Sherlocks and Dr. Watsons and try to solve mysteries using their Forensic skills. Sessions are held to help them enhance their knowledge.

Transcaper

Gadgets flagship event where the teams dove into layers of dreams, solved and built circuits while eliminating the other teams to emerge victorious. A three day event which includes a workshop. The final face off involves building a circuit from the knowledge gained during the workshop.

KOTH (King of the Hills)

IE's collaborative SIG event, where first years battle their way through the challenges set by each of our SIGs at each 'hill' to finally become 'The King'. It's basically a series of tech-based problems/games/obstacles/ puzzles.

Spark

IE Spark is NITK's own TED talk series, where we invite people mostly from our own college to come up and give talks on their areas of interest. Conducted only once or twice a year, due to its strict quality standards, IE Spark is IE's flagship non-technical event.

Vriddhi Visit

Under our social initiative, Vriddhi, we organise regular trips to nearby care homes and orphanages to contribute our part to the less priviliged. All the club members come together and spend a day with the students by teaching, playing with them.

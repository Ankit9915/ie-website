Special Interest Groups

Code

Our vision is to develop the average standard at NITK, to set standards higher for future batches, to help bring out the best in them. Code is a modest group of some of the most talented and overachieving students of NITK. We let our resumes do the talking.

Gadget

Find your Electronic labs boring and pointless? The Gadget family is a group of ‘heavily doped’ & motivated individuals who learn and propagate practical electronics by tinkering the various devices and circuits around us. In other words, the more the LEDs we burn, the more pleasure we derive.

Garage

IE Garage, is a group of, the top upcoming engineers in the core branches of engineering. Dedicated, hardworking, and sincere, we aim to solve common world problems using engineering methodology

Script

On literature appreciation, discussing the intricate and magical power of words. We also aim at making our own blog!

Vriddhi

Our social initiative, which aims at small to large scale rural development programmes in collaboration with various NGO's.

Robotics

A culmination of all SIG's, we work on everything from Core Robotics, IoT, AI that makes life easier and futuristic.

Capital

Capital is the newest special interest group (SIG) of IE NITK. We direct our efforts in sensitizing the student community towards socially directed financial and economic solutions to real world problems

Media

The newly launched Media SIG ensures that our club events do not go unnoticed by the student community, by managing the various social media profiles and the necessary publicity.

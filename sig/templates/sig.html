Special Interest Groups

Code

Our vision is to develop the average standard at NITK, to set standards higher for future batches, to help bring out the best in them. Code is a modest group of some of the most talented and overachieving students of NITK. We let our resumes do the talking.

Gadget

Find your Electronic labs boring and pointless? The Gadget family is a group of ‘heavily doped’ & motivated individuals who learn and propagate practical electronics by tinkering the various devices and circuits around us. In other words, the more the LEDs we burn, the more pleasure we derive.

Garage

IE Garage, is a group of, the top upcoming engineers in the core branches of engineering. Dedicated, hardworking, and sincere, we aim to solve common world problems using engineering methodology
